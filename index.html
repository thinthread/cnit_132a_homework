My name is Stephanie, and I am a mixed-media artist with an emphasis on photography. I am passionate about programming, and am on track to becoming a full-stack engineer. While attending Hackbright (a women's bootcamp that focuses on full-stack engineering, and uses such tech langiages/ tools as Python, Flask, PSQL and more), I realized that fine art and programming are not so different after all. Both involes creative thinking and constant innovative problem solving. While interning at HireClub as a full-stack engineer as well as staff photographer, and intering at CCSF as a Data Security Analyst, my love of programming intensified. I am anticipating completing a CS degree, and beginning a career as a full-stack engineer after earning an AS degree and Web Development Certificate. I am looking forward to bringing my unique, artistic vision to the world of programming.
