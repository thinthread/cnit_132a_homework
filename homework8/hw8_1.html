Research Methods and Tools



In this assignment, it was requested that students generate a list of keywords that coincided with words that could be found on our sites. The assignment also asked us to cross reference keywords commonly utilized in searches across the web.


At the beginning of the project, I wrote down a collection of words I had used myself when carrying out web searches. I had used these words throughout the course of the class. They were also words/techniques/tech tools that could be found throughout my site.


After I had made the list of words and terms, I did a general search of “keywords.” This led me to sites that specialized in “keyword” data collection. This data is collected in order to enable the optimization of a website. Many of these keyword data collection sites requested that I sign up initially for a free trial period, after which the site would charge a fee for their services.


Honing my search methods, I focused on Google Trends and Wordstream. I found these two keyword tool sites helpful in streamlining the collection of the keywords mentioned below.
