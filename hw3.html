Here are two images I uploaded to my site. The first image was not altered in any way. Utilizing the site https://imagecompressor.com/, I applied a -19% compression to the original image which rendered a compressed image file.

At first glance, there appears to be little, if any, difference between the two images. Upon closer examination of the altered image, the effect of the compression does become evident. The color tones of the modified image are slightly deeper, and shadow areas have darkened slightly as a result of the compression.

These minor variances would probably not be obvious to the majority of people visiting the site. In fact, after the compression, initially I did not notice any marked disparity between the two images.

Noted above are the specs for each image. You may want to take note that all parameters of the specs are identical with the exception of the kilobytes.
